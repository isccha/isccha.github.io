Employment exchange website

As part of my graduation project in engineering developed from scratch an employment exchange system using Ruby on Rails for students of electrical engineering at Universidad de Costa Rica. The webpage had the ability to let students submit their curriculum vitae and download it as PDF. Employers were able to search for students based on custom searches and publish job announcements. Incorporated gems like Paperclip, PdfWriter, RubyZip and configured Action Mailer.

The application worked for about 6 years and it had over 900 active users.
